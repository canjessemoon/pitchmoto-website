How It Works

Discover how PitchMoto revolutionizes startup-investor connections

PitchMoto is where startups and investors meet, upvote, and move innovation forward—anytime, anywhere.

  1. 1

    Founders Upload a Pitch

    Create a free account and build your startup profile. Upload your pitch deck, a short video, and your "ask" (funding, feedback, partnerships). Our step-by-step guide ensures you hit all the key points.

  2. 2

    Investors Discover & Upvote

    Accredited angels and VCs browse pitches, filter by industry, and upvote their favorites. Top-voted startups get featured in trending lists and spotlight emails, helping them get noticed faster.

  3. 3

    Engage and Connect

    Investors can ask public questions, leave comments, or (if subscribed) message founders directly. Founders get real-time feedback, analytics on views/upvotes, and direct connections with investors.

  4. 4

    Grow with the Community

    PitchMoto is always-on: No more waiting for demo day! New startups and updates appear daily. Founders can update traction, milestones, and decks as they grow. Investors can set alerts for sectors they love.
