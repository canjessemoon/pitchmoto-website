Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about PitchMoto

Who can join PitchMoto as an investor?

Any accredited angel, venture fund, or corporate innovation leader can sign up. Team plans are available for funds and firms.

How do upvotes work?

Each investor can upvote a pitch once. The most upvoted pitches trend to the top, but investors can also use filters and search to discover promising startups in any sector.

Is it really free for founders?

Yes! Founders never pay to create a profile or upload a pitch. We only charge investors for premium access and features.

What's included in the investor subscription?

Premium investors get full access to advanced filters, direct messaging, watchlists, team dashboards, and exclusive event invitations.

Can founders update their pitch after publishing?

Absolutely. Log in anytime to update your deck, video, team info, or traction.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We use industry-standard security and never share sensitive data without your consent.

How do I get featured as a top startup?

The more upvotes and engagement your pitch receives, the higher you rank on our trending list and the more likely you are to be featured in our newsletters and spotlights.

Can service providers (lawyers, accountants, agencies) join?

Not at this time. PitchMoto is currently focused on founders and investors only.

Who do I contact for support?

Reach us anytime at support@pitchmoto.com or through our contact form.
